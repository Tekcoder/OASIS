OUR COURSE CONVERSION EXPERTISE

At OASIS we have the technical expertise and talented personnel needed to make your LMS Migration and Course Conversion project fast, accurate, affordable, and easy.

Our expert team of instructional designers and e-Learning development specialists have worked with many open-source and proprietary systems and can ensure that no detail is lost in translation from the smallest nuance to the most critical features. This means that your migrated course remains remarkably similar sharing the same content, structure, functionality, and even the subtle differences like the look and feel from your original course.

Listed below are few examples of the many areas and features we can successfully migrate and some of the various LMS systems we’ve successfully worked with:

Features:

  • Assignments
  • Course Links
  • Discussion Boards
  • Question Banks
  • Quizzes / Tests
  • Course content
  • Lessons
  • Interactive games and simulations

Systems:

  • Angel
  • Blackboard WebCT
  • Sakai
  • Joule
  • Blackboard (All versions)
  • Moodle
  • Desire2Learn
But our course conversion expertise isn’t just limited to digital LMS content. We can also migrate learning content from practically any learning source including CD or paper-based courses, web-pages, and more.

Making Your Content Mobile Ready

OASIS can effectively help you transform your existing e-Learning courses into mobile compatible modules by migrating and redeveloping the content into mobile ready technologies.

From Flash to HTML5 conversion, to making existing content viewable on various screen sizes for cross-platform compatibility, OASIS has the mobile learning expertise to effectively deliver content to your learners regardless of the mobile platform they already use.

With Course Conversion +, we take your migrated course and enhance it in every way. This means we can review and tweak your instructional design to make your course more engaging and modern. We can also add and enhance images, improve the overall look and feel of your course, create interactive components and multi-media where needed, and even integrate new features not previously available in your course.

In short, Course Conversion + takes your already successful online course and turbo charges it for maximum effectiveness.
