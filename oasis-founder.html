Our Secret Sauce

  • PROFESSOR ABIOLA AWOSIKA-FAPETU

  • Position Founder

Contact Us

Obviously an academic, and a higher education administrator, she is versed in the online, brick and mortar and blended learning educational processes (Teaching, Learning, Accreditation, Quality Assurance, etc).

She has over 25 years’ experience in this field with connections in and outside the country which gives her access to quality resource people. She has spent the last seven years working in the education industry in Nigeria.

She has a vast experience in designing online and Hybrid courses for numerous platforms including Blackboard, Moodle, D2L and of course VigiLearn which she helped to design and develop. She has been teaching and designing courses in the ODL environment since 1997. She continues to design and teach online courses for the University of Wisconsin, Stout, USA, and Liberty University, Lynchburg Virginia, USA.

Professor Awosika has been working with some of Nigeria’s leading universities including Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria and Babcock University, Ilisan-Remo, to train and design courses for their ODL programmes. She has trained hundreds of Nigerian lecturers, administrators and multimedia/ICT staff on the philosophy and techniques of eLearning, across these universities including NOUN.
